# SEO playbook — Kenya

This is the working SEO reference for precifarm.com. It documents what is already wired, what we target, and what stays on the marketing/content team's monthly checklist.

The site is a static Next.js 16 export hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Every public route is indexable by default.

---

## 1. Search behaviour in Kenya — what to design for

- **Mobile-first.** ~85–90% of Kenyan web traffic is mobile (Communications Authority of Kenya). Every layout decision needs to assume a 375 px viewport and a thumb. Core Web Vitals on slow Android Chrome is the binding performance test, not desktop Lighthouse.
- **English is the default search language**, but Swahili appears in long-tail queries — `solar nyumbani`, `solar borehole pump bei`, `solar ya nyumba bei nairobi`. Keep English on-page, but include Swahili keywords in alt text and FAQ answers where natural.
- **County and town intent is huge.** "Solar installer Kitui", "solar pump Naivasha", "solar Eldoret" all convert better than national queries. Mention county and town names where honest.
- **Voice search via Google Assistant and Siri** is rising fastest among 25–40 year olds. Phrase headings as natural questions where possible (FAQ blocks help).
- **Google dominates** — Bing and DuckDuckGo are rounding errors. Optimize for Google Search and Google Discover; add Bing Webmaster only if there's bandwidth.
- **WhatsApp click-throughs matter.** Many Kenyan SMB buyers will research on Google, then DM via WhatsApp. The site already exposes `wa.me/254794702768` in the footer, contact, and Commercial pricing card — keep that visible.

### Gaps to close (by priority)

1. **`LocalBusiness` schema** is not yet emitted. Add to home or `/about` once the office address, opening hours, and geo coordinates are confirmed.
2. **`BreadcrumbList`** is not emitted on product pages. Worth adding for the `/products/{slug}` and `/products/{slug}/spec` routes.
3. **`FAQPage`** schema is not emitted on product pages even though FAQs are present. Adding it would unlock rich snippets.
4. **`WebSite` + `SearchAction`** is not declared. Optional, but cheap.
5. **OG image is the same on every page.** Per-route OG generation (satori + resvg pipeline already in deps) would dramatically improve LinkedIn / X / WhatsApp link previews. Phase 4.
6. **No hreflang yet.** Single-locale site for now (en-KE). Revisit if a Swahili variant ships.

## 4. On-page SEO checklist (per route)

When adding or editing a page, walk through this:

- [ ] `<title>` is under 60 characters, leads with the user's query intent, ends with `· Precifarm`
- [ ] Meta description is 140–160 characters, contains one verb and one number where possible
- [ ] One `<h1>` per page, matching the page's primary keyword
- [ ] `<h2>` headings use natural question or noun phrases (helps Featured Snippets)
- [ ] Every image has descriptive `alt` text — not "image of solar panel" but "An engineer installing a 550 W panel on a corrugated metal roof in Kitui"
- [ ] First 100 words mention the primary keyword and Kenya / a Kenyan town
- [ ] Internal links point both up (to product index) and across (to comparable pages)
- [ ] At least one outbound link to a high-authority Kenyan source (EPRA, KCB, Energy Act citations) where natural — signals topical relevance
- [ ] Page passes Lighthouse Performance ≥ 90 on mobile Chrome (LH CI runs in PRs)
- [ ] No layout shift on slow connections (CLS < 0.1) — images use `<Image fill>` with `sizes`
- [ ] Print stylesheet works for spec-sheet routes

## 5. Local SEO

Single-location at present (Nairobi HQ). Field engineers operate in Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Kitui, Nakuru.

### Google Business Profile (set up once)

1. Claim the listing at <https://business.google.com>
2. Use the exact business name `Precifarm AI Ltd` (matches the legal entity in the financing PDF and footer)
3. Categories: primary `Solar energy company`, secondary `Solar energy contractor`, `Solar energy equipment supplier`
4. Address: Nairobi office (confirm street + building number)
5. Service areas: enumerate all six cities — Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Kitui, Nakuru. This unlocks ranking for `solar [city]` queries even without per-city physical addresses.
6. Phone: `+254 794 702 768` (must match the website footer exactly — NAP consistency)
7. Website: `https://precifarm.com`
8. Hours: Mon–Fri 8:00–17:00 EAT (matches the contact page)
9. Photos: real installation work — at least 10. The engineering crew portrait, the Kitui rural-school solar canopy, the Family-package home install. No stock images here.
10. Q&A section: pre-seed with the same questions used in product FAQs

### NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone)

These must match exactly across:
- Site footer
- Contact page
- Google Business Profile
- Cloudflare Pages canonical
- LinkedIn company page (when set up)
- KEDA member directory (if listed)
- Any directory citations

The site currently uses `+254 794 702 768` and `sales@precifarm.com` consistently in the footer and contact page. The about page closing CTA also matches.

- **The financing partner walkthrough** is more comprehensive than typical installer sites
- **Bespoke quoting for Commercial** plus published indicative starting points hits a Goldilocks zone — better than competitors who hide all pricing

## 8. Measurement

### Tools

- **Plausible Analytics** — already wired (`NEXT_PUBLIC_PLAUSIBLE_DOMAIN` env var). Privacy-friendly, no cookies.
- **Google Search Console** — verify ownership via DNS TXT on Cloudflare. Submit `out/sitemap.xml`. Watch:
  - Index coverage (target: 100% of public routes indexed)
  - Average position for the brand cluster (should hit 1–3 within 30 days of launch)
  - Average position for product-intent terms (target: top 10 within 90 days)
  - CTR per query (low CTR + high impressions = title/description is wrong)
  - Mobile usability errors (should always be 0 — we test in CI)
- **Lighthouse CI** — runs against `out/` in PRs ([`lighthouserc.cjs`](../lighthouserc.cjs)). Performance budget set to 90.
- **Cloudflare Web Analytics** — optional secondary signal for global traffic shape

### Conversion goals to instrument in Plausible

- Outbound clicks to `wa.me/*` (WhatsApp)
- Outbound clicks to `mailto:sales@precifarm.com`
- Outbound clicks to `tel:+254794702768`
- "Get a quote" CTA clicks
- "Apply by email" CTA clicks (from `/careers`)
- "Print or save as PDF" button clicks (`/products/*/spec`)
- Outbound clicks to financing partner URLs (KCB, Equity, Stanbic, Co-op)

These are already trackable as outbound link events in Plausible — confirm the configuration after deploy.
