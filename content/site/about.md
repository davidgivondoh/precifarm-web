We are a premium Kenyan energy infrastructure company. Operating from Nairobi, our elite field engineering teams span Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Kitui, and Nakuru, delivering uncompromising power solutions.

We were founded in August 2025 to disrupt a broken industry pattern.

For too long, solar installations were transactional. Systems were deployed, and companies vanished. Years later, as batteries degraded and controllers failed, customers were left with stranded assets and a roof full of regret.

We believe critical energy infrastructure demands lifelong accountability. So we engineered a radically different approach to distributed solar.

## Three tiers, one unified platform

Traditional solar companies fracture their operations. They sell fragmented products with separate maintenance lines, disparate hardware, and inconsistent support.

We architected three enterprise-grade tiers: Starter, Family, and Commercial. All three operate on a singular, unified data layer, serviced by the same elite engineers. A homeowner upgrading from Starter to Family experiences zero friction. A commercial facility running Commercial relies on the same robust dashboard that powers our entire network.

## A data layer on every system

Every system we install carries an integrated data layer — a small edge board on the charge controller that reads voltage, current, temperature, and state of charge every minute and transmits to our cloud platform.

We see drift before customers see darkness. Most faults are caught and resolved before anyone notices. When something does fail, our service team arrives with the right part, because the system told us what it was before they left the warehouse.

## Beyond the Commercial tier — BESS consultancy and EPC

For projects too large for the standard Commercial system — typically 50 kWh to 10 MWh of storage — we run a consultancy and EPC services arm on a quotation basis. Site assessment, system design, BOM specification, EPRA paperwork, procurement, installation, commissioning, and a five-year service envelope, end to end. Industrial cold storage, agro-processing facilities, hospital backup, telecom edge sites, irrigation cooperatives, lodges. The same engineers who service the Neura Pod fleet run these jobs, and every system reports through the same data layer.
