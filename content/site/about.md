We are a premium Kenyan energy infrastructure company. Operating from Nairobi, our elite field engineering teams span Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, Kitui, and Nakuru, delivering uncompromising power solutions.

We launched in 2024 to disrupt a broken industry pattern.

For too long, solar installations were transactional. Systems were deployed, and companies vanished. Years later, as batteries degraded and controllers failed, customers were left with stranded assets and a roof full of regret.

We believe critical energy infrastructure demands lifelong accountability. So we engineered a radically different approach to distributed solar.

## Three tiers, one unified platform

Traditional solar companies fracture their operations. They sell fragmented products with separate maintenance lines, disparate hardware, and inconsistent support.

We architected three enterprise-grade tiers: Starter, Family, and Business. All three operate on a singular, unified telemetry layer, serviced by the same elite engineers. A homeowner upgrading from Starter to Family experiences zero friction. A commercial facility running Business relies on the same robust dashboard that powers our entire network.

## Software that watches

Every system we install has a small board on the charge controller. We call it PreciSense. It reports voltage, current, temperature, and state of charge to our platform every minute.

We see drift before customers see darkness. Most faults are caught and resolved before anyone notices. When something does fail, our service team arrives with the right part, because the system told us what it was before they left the warehouse.

## Field engineers, not subcontractors

We are growing on revenue. We do not have outside investors. We do not have a five-year plan that depends on someone else writing a cheque.

We pay our team well, we pay our suppliers on time, and we treat our customers like the next ten will come from them telling someone.
