## Complete off-grid freedom for your home.
The **Precifarm Family** is built around the Neura Pod 5 — a 5 kWh LiFePO4 battery and a 3 kW hybrid inverter, fed by four 550 W panels (2.2 kWp). Sized to serve a 2- to 3-bedroom home drawing about 4 kWh per day, with one-day battery refill even in worst-case Kenyan irradiance.

Fridge, TV, Wi-Fi, fans, full lighting, and the occasional iron all run as if you were on the grid — except the meter never ticks up. The Neura Smart Energy Manager prioritises essentials when the battery is low, so the home keeps running through any cloudy stretch.
