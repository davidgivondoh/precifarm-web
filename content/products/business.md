## Commercial-grade power for 24/7 operations.
The **Precifarm Commercial** is built around the Neura Pod 15 — a 15 kWh LiFePO4 stack and a 12 kW hybrid inverter, fed by twenty-four 550 W panels (13.2 kWp). The most common deployment is **borehole irrigation**: the 12 kW inverter handles a 3HP submersible pump's startup surge with comfortable headroom, and we engineer voltage-drop budgets so the pump can sit up to **3 km from the water source** while still driving drip or sprinkler irrigation across **5 to 45 acres**.

Other typical loads: cold rooms, milk-cooling stations, milling, agro-processing, clinics, schools, SACCOs, lodges, and SMEs with cold storage, PoS, networking, and security around the clock. Tariff-aware energy management uses solar and battery first, grid only when it pays.

Because no two commercial sites are alike — distance, acreage, load profile, terrain — pricing is **confirmed after a site survey** rather than sold as a flat sticker price. Contact us to start the conversation.
