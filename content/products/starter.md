## Power your essentials, uninterrupted.
The **Precifarm Starter** is the entry point to the Precifarm platform: a single 550 W panel feeding a Neura Pod 1 with a 1.5 kWh LiFePO4 battery and a 1.5 kW hybrid inverter — sized to refill the battery in one good solar day while serving your daily load.

Honest engineering, no undersizing. Lights, Wi-Fi, TV, fans, and phone charging stay on through any blackout, and the Neura AI watches the system around the clock so faults are caught before they become outages.
